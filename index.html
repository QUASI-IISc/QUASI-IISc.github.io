    About Us

    'Queer and Straight Allies at IISC' or QUASI is student initiative: a collective of queer(LGBTQ+) individuals and straight allies at IISc and sister institutes like NIAS, NCBS, JNCASR.

    Vision

    Make the campus an amiable and inclusive space for the whole queer community by acting as a platform for raising awareness about sexual and gender diversity.

    Aim

    QUASI aims to provide a support system for LGBTQI+ individuals who might be stigmatized due to their identity and help them in their struggle.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Who can join QUASI?

    Any queer and straight allies at IISc or sister institutes like NIAS, NCBS, JNCASR are welcome to join us.

    Are there UGs in the group?

    A significant fraction of QUASI are UGs. In fact, you will find that UGs are the most active members of this community.

    Is it a group for dating or flings?

    No, QUASI is not a group for dating or flings. It is a platform for queer individuals and straight allies to raise awareness and support the queer community.

    Is QUASI an LGBTQ+ rights group?

    No, QUASI is not an LGBTQ+ rights group. It is a collective to provide a formal support system to queer students and engage in dialogue concerning gender and sexuality.

    What are the activities done by the group?

    QUASI hosts queer movie/documentary screenings, panel discussions, talks and give talks at various events. Furthermore, IIScQ conducts weekly meetings where the members could share their experiences and let of some steam with fun games.

    Is there an exclusive queer group? Will my identity be protected?

    IIScQ is a part of QUASI exclusively for queer individuals.
    Your right to protect your identity is important for us. We will be discrete about your identity.
